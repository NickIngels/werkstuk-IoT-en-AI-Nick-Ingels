
Fruit en groenten detectie - v2 2021-05-11 12:18pm
==============================

This dataset was exported via roboflow.ai on May 11, 2021 at 10:19 AM GMT

It includes 114 images.
Groenten-en-fruit are annotated in Tensorflow TFRecord (raccoon) format.

The following pre-processing was applied to each image:
* Auto-orientation of pixel data (with EXIF-orientation stripping)
* Resize to 512x512 (Stretch)

No image augmentation techniques were applied.
